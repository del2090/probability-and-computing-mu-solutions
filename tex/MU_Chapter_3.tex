\section*{3.1}
$\textbf{E}[X^2]=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\frac{1}{n}\times i^2 = \frac{(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}$, and $\textbf{E}[X]=\frac{n+1}{2}$.\\
Thus, $\textbf{Var}[X]=\textbf{E}[X^2]-(\textbf{E}[X])^2=\frac{n^2-1}{12}$.
